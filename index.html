About me

I am currently an Assistant Professor of Mechanical Engineering at the R.B. Annis School of Engineering at the University of Indianapolis , Indianapolis, IN. Before joining UIndy, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the School of Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University , West Lafayette, IN. From 2015 to 2017, I was a Postdoctoral Reseach Associate at the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Texas Tech University , Lubbock, Tx.

Research

I am interested in solving fluid dynamics problems related to energy, environment, and biology by numerical simulations.
